FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How can Visionary detect health conditions through my eyes?
Visionary utilizes advanced AI algorithms to analyze detailed images of your eyes, identifying subtle indicators and patterns that may be associated with various health conditions.
Is the analysis process safe and non-invasive?
Yes, the analysis is completely safe and non-invasive. It typically involves capturing images of your eyes using standard imaging techniques. There is no direct contact or any harmful procedures involved.
What types of health conditions can Visionary potentially detect?
Visionary is being developed to potentially detect a range of health conditions. Our research is ongoing, and we aim to provide information on the specific conditions we can analyze as our technology advances.
Is Visionary a substitute for traditional medical diagnosis?
No, Visionary is intended to be a supportive tool and not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Any findings should always be discussed with a qualified healthcare provider for proper evaluation and guidance.
