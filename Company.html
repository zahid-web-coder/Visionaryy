About Visionary

We are a health-tech company transforming eye care through accessible AI-powered diagnostics. Our mission is to empower communities, improve early detection, and reduce preventable blindness globally.

Our Mission

To democratize access to retinal diagnostics using cutting-edge artificial intelligence and mobile tools.

Our Vision

A world where everyone, regardless of geography or income, can access early eye disease detection.

Our Values

Equity, innovation, transparency, and collaboration are at the core of every product and partnership we build.

Our Team

A cross-disciplinary team of technologists, clinicians, and researchers united to solve global health disparities.
